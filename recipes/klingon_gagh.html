Gagh

Fresh Gagh

Gagh was a Klingon delicacy made from serpent worms. Although most Klingons preferred to consume it alive, it could also be served stewed or cold.

Ingredients

  • Gagh (Sea Serpent)

Steps

  1. Get your sea worms
  2. Bring worms to boil in water
  3. After a few minutes (not long enough to kill but just long enough to make angry) scoop Gagh into bowl
  4. Bon appetit!
